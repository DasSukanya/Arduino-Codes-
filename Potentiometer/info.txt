Using a potentiometer, you will be able to control the resistance of an LED.  Turning the knob will increase and decrease the frequency the LED blinks.

Parts Needed

    (1) Arduino Uno
    (1) USB A-to-B Cable
    (1) Breadboard – Half Size
    (1) LED 5mm
    (1) 220 Ω Resistor
    (1) Potentiometer (10k Trimpot)
    (6) Jumper Wires
